Today we’re announcing the release of several enhancements to deepen the security investigation capabilities of the Workspace audit log, including expanded fields across many data sources.<div><br /></div><div>These new enhancements include:</div><div><br /></div><div><ol style="text-align: left;"><li>Introduction of owner details for resource attribute</li><li>Expansion of resource and actor attributes to additional data sources</li><li>Introduction of new device info attribute for multiple data sources</li></ol></div><div><b><br /></b></div><div><b>New owner details for enhanced resource visibility in Security Investigation Tool and Audit logs</b></div><div><br /></div><div>We’re adding a new “Owner details” field to the “Resources” attribute, making it easier to identify who owns a resource during security investigations. This field uses two primary components:</div><div><br /></div><div><ul style="text-align: left;"><li><b>Owner Type: </b>Specifies the category of the owner, which can be an individual person (User), the entire organization (Customer), or a Group.</li><li><b>Owner Identity: </b>Contains specific details, such as IDs or email addresses, of that owner.</li></ul></div><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
