In October 2025, we launched a beta for Organize My Files in Drive. This feature is now generally available and has started rolling out to eligible Google Workspace and Google AI plans . It can be overwhelming to see loose files in your Drive and moving them into the right folder can be manual, tedious, and repetitive.
