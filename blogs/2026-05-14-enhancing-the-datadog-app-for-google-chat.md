The latest version of the Datadog app for Google Chat allows you to integrate Datadog into your Google Chat workflows more seamlessly and stay on top of critical infrastructure without leaving the flow of team collaboration. Newly added features include: Link previews Cut and paste a widget from a Datadog dashboard and see the chart inside Google Chat. Improved notification set-up Link your Workspace domain to Datadog and you’ll be able to easily create Google Chat notification handles from within Datadog.
